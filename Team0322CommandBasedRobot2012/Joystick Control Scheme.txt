DRIVE_STICK
Left Side Drive = Left Stick Y Axis
Right Side Drive = Right Stick Y Axis

Brake Control = Button 6 (R1)
Precision Drive System = [While Holding Button 1(X)] Left Stick (Arcade Style)


MANIPULATOR_STICK
Bridge Manipulator Forward = Button 1(X)
Bridge Manipulator Rearward = Button 2(Y)

Bottom Roller In = Button 2(A)
Bottom Roller Out = Button 3(B)
